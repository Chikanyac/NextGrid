5 Tips to Keep Your PC Running Smoothly

Regular maintenance can drastically improve your computer's performance. With a few simple steps, you can clean your hardware, update your software, and optimize startup programs.

1. Clean Your Hardware

Dust and debris block airflow and can cause overheating. Wipe down your PC, use compressed air on fans, and ensure all components are dust-free.

2. Update Your Software

Keep your operating system, drivers, and apps up to date. Remove software you no longer use to free resources.

3. Optimize Startup Programs

Too many programs launching at startup slows your PC. Disable unnecessary startup items via Task Manager (Windows) or Login Items (Mac).

4. Manage Disk Space

Delete temporary files and old downloads. DUninstall apps you no longer use.

5. Monitor Performance

Use Task Manager (Windows) or Activity Monitor (Mac) to check CPU, RAM, and disk usage. Identify apps that slow down your PC and take action.

Bonus Tips

  • Run disk cleanup to free space.
  • Scan regularly for viruses and malware.
  • Back up your data to avoid loss.

Regular maintenance keeps your PC running fast and extends its lifespan. Clean it, update it, and optimize it for best results.
